Infinito Designs

Designer and Developer

The Process

Infinito Designs was the first of many group projects. The goal was to build a website for us as if we were an agency. This project gave us a chance to collaborate with others as well as get us being creative with javascript and new design techniques we may have never used before.

The Result

By the end we have a functioning one page scroll site with beautiful vibrant colours and fun features. Although it provided us with the challenges that can come with working with others it also helped learn and grow from the experience.
